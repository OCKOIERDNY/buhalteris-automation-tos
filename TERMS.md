Docs that formats weekly meeting protocol documents, applies numbering and
colour rules, archives tasks to an archive document, and sends protocol and
reminder emails on the user's behalf. By installing or using the add-on you

## 2. What the add-on does

- Reads and edits Google Docs you open it in, and Docs in a Drive folder you
  configure (formatting, numbering, archiving).
- Creates protocol and archive documents on your request.
- Sends emails (protocol notifications, reminders, per-person and deadline
  messages) from your Google account, only when you trigger an action or enable
  the optional automatic schedule.
- Stores its settings (folder ID, selected documents, team list, email
  templates, schedule toggles) in the script's properties within your Google

A "test mode" (DRY_RUN) is provided that logs intended emails instead of
sending them. You are responsible for turning it off when you want real emails
